It all started with a dream...

Victor and Trudy Smith worked for years in the insurance industry. Their dream had always been to open a high end Bed and Breakfast of their own.

The Safety Harbor Bed & Breakfast has four luxury rooms available for those who want to experience a world class vacation in beautiful Safety Harbor.

Spend a day at nearby Clearwater Beach. Get a massage at the famous Safety Harbor Spa. Shop and Dine just steps away from your front door.

In the mornings, enjoy a gourmet breakfast and in the the evenings, you can attend our social hour with fine wines and hors d'oeuvre's.

Book now and enjoy our little slice of Heaven!
